1. Who We Are

Rundom ("we", "our", or "us") is a location-based running game developed and operated as an independent application. The app is available at rundom-app.pages.dev and distributed via the Google Play Store.

This Privacy Policy is governed by and compliant with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

2. Information We Collect

Data When collected Why
GPS / Location Only while the app is open and you have pressed START To record your running route and create territory polygons
Username When you first open the app (you choose it yourself) To label your territories on the map
Territory data Each time you complete a run GPS path, area, distance, duration — used to draw your territory
Anonymous player ID On first launch (randomly generated) To distinguish your territories from other users' in multiplayer
Run statistics Each time you complete a run Distance, time, pace — displayed in your personal stats

We do not collect: real name, email address, phone number, payment information, contacts, photos, or any other personal data beyond what is listed above.

Location data is collected only in the foreground — the app does not track your location when closed or running in the background.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your running territories on the map
  • To calculate and show your personal running statistics (distance, pace, area captured)
  • To show your territories to other Rundom users on the global map (if Firebase is configured)
  • To track achievements and personal records
  • To distinguish you from other players in multiplayer rooms

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the game.

4. Data Storage & Retention

Local storage (your device): All run records, achievements, and settings are stored in your browser's localStorage. This data stays on your device and is never sent anywhere unless you use the global map feature.

Firebase (remote, optional): If the global territory map feature is enabled, your territory GPS paths, username, and anonymous player ID are stored in Google Firebase Realtime Database. This data remains until you clear your records in the app or request deletion.

Deletion: You can delete all locally stored data at any time via Settings → Clear All Records in the app. To request deletion of Firebase data, contact us (see Section 10).

6. Cross-Border Data Transfers

Rundom is operated from Australia. When you use the global map feature, your territory data is stored on Google Firebase servers, which are primarily located in the United States. By using this feature, you consent to your data being transferred to and stored in the United States.

Google LLC participates in data transfer frameworks and applies appropriate safeguards under their Privacy Policy. This transfer is made in accordance with Australian Privacy Principle 8 (APP 8).

7. Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data from our Firebase database
  • Opt out — stop using the app and clear all local data at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the details in Section 10. We will respond within 30 days.

If you believe we have breached your privacy rights, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

8. Children's Privacy

Rundom is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

For significant changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify users through the app.
